Director Howard navigates the action sequences in an exciting manner and gets the most out his talented cast. Ehrenreich has the tough task of stepping into an iconic role, but succeeds in making the role his own, while still paying tribute to what made Han so likable. The narrative has its flaws, but thanks to the cast, action, and humor injected by Howard, this is an overall entertaining and fast-paced adventure.
